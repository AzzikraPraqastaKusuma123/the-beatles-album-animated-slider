i want to hold your hand
"I Want to Hold Your Hand" is a Beatles song written by John Lennon and Paul McCartney. Recorded on October 17, 1963, it was released on November 29, 1963. The song quickly sold over a million copies in the UK and reached number one, staying on the charts for 21 weeks.

Yesterday
"Yesterday" is an acoustic ballad by The Beatles from the album Help! (1965), written by Paul McCartney and credited to Lennon/McCartney. The song, which reflects on a broken relationship, is recognized by Guinness World Records as the most covered pop song. Despite its popularity, it was never released as a single in the UK, as it was rejected by the other three band members.
